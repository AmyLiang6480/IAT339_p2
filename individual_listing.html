3RD STUDIO ALBUM FROM TEXAS INSTRUMENTAL PROG METAL QUARTET, Polyphia! For New Levels New Devils, the crew continue honing their synthesis of trap beats, guitar arpeggios, slick bass guitar pops and chameleonic time signatures to forge a unique sound that's somewhere between math rock (but less sex-deprived), fusion, the crunchiness of Justice and Chicano rock, with a surprise around every corner. Fans of Toro Y Moi will love "So Strange," featuring Cuco on vox; also check "Death Note" (feat. Ichika), "Saucy," "GOAT" and "Drown" (featuring Mateus Asato).
