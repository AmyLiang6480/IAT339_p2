About Us - Spin City Records

Our Mission

At Spin City Records, we're passionate about vinyl records and the unique listening experience they offer. Our mission is to curate a collection of high-quality vinyl records that spans genres and eras, from classic rock and jazz to indie and hip hop. We believe that music is meant to be experienced in the physical world, and that there's something special about putting on a record, dropping the needle, and immersing yourself in the sound.

We also aim to create a welcoming and inclusive community around vinyl culture, offering events, workshops, and resources to help people learn more about the history and art of vinyl records.

Above all, we're committed to sharing our love of music with others and helping people discover new artists and sounds that inspire and move them. We believe that vinyl records are a vital part of the cultural landscape, and we're proud to be a part of this enduring and dynamic medium.

Our History

Spin City Records was founded in 2019 by Julia Patel, a lifelong music lover and vinyl enthusiast. Julia had always been drawn to the warm, rich sound of vinyl records, and had amassed a large collection of records over the years.

In 2019, Julia decided to turn their passion for vinyl into a business, and opened Spin City Records in Vancouver. The store quickly gained a reputation as a go-to destination for vinyl enthusiasts, thanks to its carefully curated selection of records and knowledgeable staff.

As the business grew, Julia realized the potential of expanding Spin City Records' reach beyond the physical store. In 2022, Spin City Records launched its online store, allowing customers from around the world to browse and purchase vinyl records from the comfort of their homes.

Since then, the online store has become an integral part of Spin City Records' business, with a wide selection of vinyl records, competitive prices, and fast, reliable shipping. We're proud to offer customers a seamless online shopping experience, with easy navigation, secure payment options, and responsive customer service.

Today, Spin City Records is a thriving and dynamic business, with a loyal following of vinyl lovers and a commitment to keeping the vinyl tradition alive and well. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a curious newcomer, we invite you to join us on this journey and discover the joy of vinyl records for yourself.
